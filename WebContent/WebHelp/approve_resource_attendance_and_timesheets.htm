Approve resource shift check-ins and timesheets

The test manager of a product verifies and approves the shift check-ins, and the test lead of the work package can approves timesheet entries of the resource.

 

Approve resource shift check-ins

To approve the resource attendance, follow these steps:

  1. Click My Tasks > Shift Approvals. On the Products pane, select Test Factory > Product.
  2. On the Resource Shift Approval tab, in the Pick a Date field, select a date.
  3. In the Status field, select a status, and in the Shift field, select a shift.
  4. Select the Approved checkbox to approve the shift check-in time.

Approve resource timesheets

To approve the resource timesheet, follow these steps:

  1. Click My Tasks > Approve Timesheets. On the Work Packages pane, select the Product > Build.
  2. On the Approve Timesheet Entries pane, select the Approve Activity checkbox to approve the timesheet entries of the resource.
  3. If required, in the Approver comments field, enter comments.
