Introduction

integrated Life Cycle Management (iLCM) is a service delivery platform to support Dashboard and Reporting, Activity Life Cylce, and Test Life Cycle. In the Test Life cycle process, iLCM supports Test Factory Model and Regular Test Engagements.

 

 

 

Overview of iLCM

iLCM supports testing lifecycle and activity management workflow in different engagement models. iLCM facilitates product management, resource management, and test and activity work package management.
