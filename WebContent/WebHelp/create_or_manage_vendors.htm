Create Vendor Accounts

The admin of the test center can create or manage the vendor account details such as single point of contact person's name, e-mail address, and contact number.

 

Create a vendor account

To create a vendor account, follow these steps:

  1. Click Admin > Manage Vendors. As a result, the Vendors page displays the list of available vendors.

  2. Click the Add button, and enter the vendor account details such as company name, contact name, email address, and contact number.

  3. Select or clear the Status check box to enable or disable the vendor account.

  4. Click the Save button to create a vendor account.
