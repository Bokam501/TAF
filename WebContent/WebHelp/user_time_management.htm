User Time Management

Overview

The User Time Management screen allows you to record multiple shift check-ins and check-outs, daily aestivates in timesheet, and to mark user availability for a shift on a specific day. You can register multiple shift check-in and check-outs for a day based on the allocated work packages. To register a second shift check-in for a day, you must register the shift check-out for the previous or first shift check-in.

To access this page, login as resource, and click My Tasks > Time Management. Click tab links for level information:

  • My Shift Check-Ins and Time Bookings
  • My Availability

My Shift Check-Ins and Time Bookings

This tab allows you to register the check-in and check-out time, and also to fill activities for the day in the timesheet.

Fields

Field

Description

Monthly Calendar

Displays the current month. Based on the entered shift check-in hours and timesheet hours of a day, dates in the calendar are highlighted in different colors. For example, the resource registers the shift check-in for a day as 8 hours (Check-In: 08:00, Check-Out: 16:00) on 29. Then the 29th day in the calendar is highlight in green ().

  • Entered timesheet hour and check-in hour are equal
  • Entered timesheet hour is not equal to check-in hour
  • Shift check-in is not registered
  • No activity is performed

 

Current/Selected Date

Displays the current date or the date selected from the calendar.

My Availability

This tab allows the resource to enter the availability for a specific shift of the day. Based on the resource availability, the test manager books and reserves the resources for the day. The My Booking(s) and Reservation(s) pane displays the booking and reservation details of the resource. In the My Booking(s) and Reservation(s) pane, B denotes the resource is booked, and R denotes the resource is reserved for that shift of the day.
