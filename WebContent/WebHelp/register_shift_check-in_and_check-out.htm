Register Shift Check-in and Check-out

The shift check-in and check-out is performed by the resource to record the daily activities of the resource in the timesheet.

Register shift check-in

To register the check-in time of a work shift, follow these steps:

  1. Click My Tasks > Time Management.

  2. On the My Shift Check-Ins and Time Bookings tab, select a date from the calendar the work shift.

  3. On the My Shift Check-Ins pane, click the Add button. The My Shift Check In page is displayed.

  4. Select the shift, product, and the work shift date and time, and then enter the remarks for the work shift.

  5. Click the Check In button to record the work shift check-in time.

Register shift check-out

To register the check-out time of a work shift, follow these steps:

  1. Click My Tasks > Time Management.

  2. On the My Shift Check-Ins and Time Bookings tab, select a date from the calendar the work shift.

  3. On the My Shift Check-Ins pane, click the Add button. The My Shift Check In page is displayed.

  4. Select the shift, product, and the work shift date and time, and then enter the remarks for the work shift.

  5. Click the Check Out button to record the work shift check-out time.
    Note: The check-out can be registered only when the check-in time is already registered.
