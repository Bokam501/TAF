Fill My Availability

This form allows the resource to enter the availability for a specific shift of the day. Based on the resource availability, the test manager books and reserves the resources for the day. The My Booking(s) and Reservation(s) pane displays the booking and reservation details of the resource. In the My Booking(s) and Reservation(s) pane, B denotes the resource is booked, and R denotes the resource is reserved for that shift of the day.
